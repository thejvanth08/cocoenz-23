Nostalgia - Rules

Event overview

"Nostalgia Returns is a captivating symposium event that serves as a delightful haven for fans of anime,cartoons and movies. This gathering is a celebration of the cherished past, offering a unique opportunity to relive the magic of beloved animated shows. With an array of engaging quizzes, it's a place where you can come together and test their knowledge in friendly quiz competitions. Join us for an unforgettable journey down memory lane!"

Event summary It consist of 3 rounds:
1. Quiz based on Emoji’s and Images
2. Quiz based on song’s and OST’s
3. Oral quiz based on the Theme

Team Requirements:

1. Each team can consist of a maximum of 3 members.
