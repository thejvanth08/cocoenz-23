COCOENZ'23

Narrato - Rules

Event overview

"Narrato is an exciting and intellectually stimulating paper presentation event where participants can showcase their research, knowledge, and presentation skills. This event aims to foster academic and professional development while encouraging innovative thinking and communication."

Rules and Regulations 1. Each presentation should be limited to 5 minutes, followed by a Q&A session.
2. All participants are required to submit their papers and PowerPoint presentations to cocoenz@sairamit.edu.in no later than 28.10.2023.
3. The PowerPoint presentation should consist of a maximum of 15 slides.
4. Participants are encouraged to dress professionally and present in a respectful and engaging manner.

Team Requirements:

1. Each presentation team must consist a maximum of 4 members.
