BE THE CEO - Rules

Event overview

If you are not willing to risk the usual, you will have to settle for the Ordinary”. Welcome to “Be the CEO”! This Fabulous two round competition invites the Future Entrepreneurs to showcase and enhance their ideas by their skills to their Dignitaries.

Event summary

Round 1: In the 1st Round,Participants have their freedom to choose and showcase their innovative product (software and hardware) and should explain the use of it in Industries which haven’t been used before . This round not only evaluates the product that you have chosen but also the ability to align with industry needs. One which fulfils the need will secure a spot in the next round.

Round 2: In the 2nd Round, The Industries downfall situation has been given to every team who secure spot in 2nd round .Participants has to frame a solution to overcome those circumstances. 30 minutes will be given for each team to prepare their Presentation(PPT) and should present in front of Dignitaries.

Team Requirements:

1. Each team consists of maximum 3 members
